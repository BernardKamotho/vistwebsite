About Creamy Dreams

Learn about our passion for creating the perfect yogurt experience.

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Maria and Carlos Rodriguez, Creamy Dreams began as a small family business with a simple mission: to create the most delicious and healthy yogurt using traditional methods and the finest ingredients.

What started in our home kitchen has grown into a beloved local brand, but we've never forgotten our roots. Every batch of yogurt is still made with the same attention to detail and passion for quality that inspired us from the beginning.

Our Process

We source our milk from local organic farms within a 50-mile radius, ensuring freshness and supporting our community. Our yogurt is made using traditional fermentation methods, allowing the natural probiotics to develop fully for maximum health benefits.

Each flavor is carefully crafted using real fruits, pure honey, and natural ingredients. We never use artificial preservatives, colors, or flavors because we believe nature provides the best taste.

Our Commitment

We're committed to sustainability, health, and community. Our packaging is recyclable, our ingredients are responsibly sourced, and we donate 5% of our profits to local food banks to help fight hunger in our community.
